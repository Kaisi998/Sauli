Sauli Luxury Bus as an adventurous bus

The need of Sauli Luxury Bus came about as a result of urgency from the passengers requiring to arrive early to their destinations

The need was just so huge to the extent of introducing a new route from dar-es-salaam to Tunduma since passengers loved their services

The price from Dar-es-salaam to Tunduma is 49000/= with two buses handling the service T 548 DWL and T 418 DWF

The Dar-es-salaam to Mbeya buses are respectively with registrations T 226 DPC, T 685 DPN, T 300 DRH and T 668 DTF
